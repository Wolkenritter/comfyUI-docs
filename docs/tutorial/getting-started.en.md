## FAQ

### Q: What config needed?

**A**:
- GPU: NVIDIA, 6GB VRAM minimum
- OS: Windows 10/11
- Storage: 20GB+ free space

### Q: Chinese interface?

**A**: ComfyUI is mainly English. Use browser translation.

### Q: Where to download models?

**A**:
- [Civitai](https://civitai.com)
- [Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/models)
- [ModelScope](https://www.modelscope.cn/home)

### Q: Slow generation?

**A**:
- Upgrade GPU
- Use smaller models
- Reduce sampling steps
